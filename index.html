Veteran Friendly Policies

Timeline of the introduction of policies.

  • Veteran Overmedication & Suicide Prevention Act (H.R. 67)

    Jan 04, 2021

    This act would require the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) to review the deaths of all veterans it treated who died by suicide or from a drug overdose in the last five years. The new data generated by the bill will be used to better treat veterans suffering from both mental and physical injuries.

  • May 17, 2023

    True Choice Act

    This act expands access to healthcare options for Veterans allowing them to use community care providers if the Veterans Affairs(VA) facility in their community does not offer services they need.

  • June 13,2023

    Major Richard Star Act 118th congress

    Allows a Veteran with combat-related disability & fewer than 20 years of creditable service to receive retirement pay, without reduction, concurrent with disability compensation.

  • July 26, 2023

    Sgt. 1st Class Heath Robinson Honoring Our PACT Act of 2022, Promise to Address Comprehensive Toxics (PACT)

    This act is a new law that expands (VA) Veterans Affairs Healthcare & benefits for Veteran exposed to burn pits, Agent Orange, and other toxic substances.
